Access the Part Data window

The Part Data window allows you to access all the relevant part information, such as the part description, the part number and the price (if available).

In addition, the Part Data window will indicate if replacement part information is available.

 

To access the Part Data window:

  • Click the part description in the Parts Data Grid.
  • Click the callout in the image.

 

From the Part Data window you can:

  • View all the part information such as the part number or part validity dates.
  • Click the supersession indicator to access replacement part information.
  • Edit the quantity of a part in the Qty box.
  • Click Order to add the part to the Order List.
  • Click the icon to transfer the part to the DMS.
  • Click the icons to move to the next or previous part in the Parts Data Grid.
