Change settings

Microcat allows you to view and change settings. Different settings will be available based on your manufacturer.

To access the Settings window, click Settings at the top of the screen.

 

General Settings

The General Settings tab consists of options such as:

  • Price Display Selection: Allows you to set the default price level.
  • Print and Email: Allows you to add a header or footer to printouts or emails and hide part numbers from a quote or invoice.
  • Show or Hide Non-applicable: Allows you to show or hide non-applicable sections and parts.
  • Date Format: Allows you to set the date format.

 

Personal Settings

The Personal Settings tab consists of options such as:

  • Language Selection: Allows you to select the application language and the parts description language.
  • Hotspots: Allows you to show the applicability of parts by displaying a box around the callouts on an image. For example, a green box for correct parts.
  • Colours: Allows you to change the colours that display for correct, incorrect and selective fit parts.
  • Sounds: Allows you to play a sound when ordering a part.
  • Show or Hide Screen Tips: Allows you to show or hide the screen tips.

 

Account Details

The Account Details tab displays your company and personal details based on your Microcat subscription. If you need to change your company details, contact the Licence Administrator of your Microcat subscription.

 

Integration Settings

The options in the Integration Settings tab will vary based on your manufacturer.

The Integration Settings tab consists of options such as:

  • Third-party part locators: Allows you to change the login information for third-party part locators such as EDIX, PartsVoice and D2DLink. Right-click on a part in the Parts Data Grid to access the part locator.
  • DMSi integration: Allows you to change the port setting between the DMSi and Microcat and to show or hide the status bar at the bottom of the screen.

 

For assistance with integrating Microcat with your DMS, contact Customer Service.

For more information on DMS integration options, refer to the DMS Integration Guide.
