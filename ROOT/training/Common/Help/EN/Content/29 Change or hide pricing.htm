Change or hide pricing

The Orders screen allows you to change the price level applied to parts on an Order List. In addition, you can hide the price of parts on the Order List. Hiding the price of parts is useful when confirming a specific part directly with a customer.

The availability of price information and price levels will vary based on your manufacturer.

 

To change the price level:

  1. Display an Order List.
  2. Select a price level from the Price drop-down list.

The selected price level will be applied to all parts in the Order List.

 

To hide the price of parts:

  1. Display an Order List.
  2. Select Hide Pricing from the Price drop-down list.

The pricing columns will be hidden on the Order List. In addition, the pricing will be hidden from the Parts Data Grid and the Part Data window.

  1. To show pricing, select a price level from the Price drop-down list.

 

To view your price display options, click General Settings from the Settings menu.
