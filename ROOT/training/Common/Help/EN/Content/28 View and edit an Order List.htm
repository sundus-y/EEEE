View and edit an Order List

The Orders screen is where you can view and edit an Order List.

Microcat allows you to change the sequence of parts on the Order List to meet a customer’s preferences. This saves time and provides a professional service.

 

To delete a line:

  1. Click the icon on the line you wish to remove.
  2. Click Yes.

 

To return to the image:

  1. Click the icon on the required line.

The image containing the selected part will be displayed.

  1. To return to the Orders screen, click Orders at the top of the screen.

 

To edit the quantity of a part:

  1. Click the Qty box.
  2. Type the quantity into the Qty box.

 

To change the sequence of parts on the Order List, click and drag a line to the required position.

To reverse the list of parts on the Order List, click Sort.
