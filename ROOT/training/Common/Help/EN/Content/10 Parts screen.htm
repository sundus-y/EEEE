Parts screen

The Parts screen is used to locate parts, add parts to the Order List and transfer parts to your Dealer Management System (DMS).

When you identify a vehicle the Parts screen will be displayed automatically. To access the Parts screen at any time, click Parts at the top of the screen.

 

From the Parts screen you can:

  • Search for parts using a range of search types
  • Navigate the index using the graphic or text view
  • Use the quick navigation tool
  • Display images in a variety of ways
  • Work with the Parts Data Grid
  • Add user notes to a section or part
  • Add parts to the Order List
  • Transfer a part to your DMS

 

Press F11 on the keyboard to display a full-screen view of Microcat.
