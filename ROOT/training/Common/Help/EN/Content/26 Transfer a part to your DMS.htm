Transfer a part to your DMS

Microcat allows you to transfer a single part to your Dealer Management System (DMS). The Infomedia DMSi application must be installed and configured before transferring parts to your DMS.

From the Part Data window you can transfer a part directly to your DMS without first sending it to the Order List.

 

To transfer a part directly from the Part Data window to your DMS:

  1. Click the part description or the callout in the image.

The Part Data window will be displayed.

  1. Click the icon in the Part Data window.

If your DMS is not running, you will be prompted to launch the application. Then, your DMS screen and the Transfer List window will be displayed.

  1. In the Transfer List window, select the part and click Transfer.

The Transfer List window contains the part you want to transfer.

 

For assistance with integrating Microcat with your DMS, contact Customer Service.

For more information on DMS integration options, refer to the DMS Integration Guide.
