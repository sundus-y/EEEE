Navigate the index using a text view

The Graphic Index can also be displayed as a text-based view.

 

To navigate the index using a text view:

  1. Click the icon to display a text view.
  2. Click a major section from the index.
  3. Click a minor section from the index.
  4. Continue to navigate through the index to the image.
  5. From the image you can:
  • Double-click a callout to add a part to the Order List.
  • Click a callout to display the Part Data window.
  • Click the Illustrated Parts tab to view the list of parts in the image.

 

To go back a level of the index, click the icon.
