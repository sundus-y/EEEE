Save and retrieve Customer Tags

Customer Tags allow you to save a unique name for a specific vehicle and are useful for frequent customers such as fleet customers.

 

To save a Customer Tag:

  1. Identify a vehicle by VIN.
  2. Type the Customer Tag into the Customer Tag box that is located in the Vehicle Information panel.
  3. Click Save.

 

To retrieve a Customer Tag:

  1. Type the saved Customer Tag into the Customer Tag box that is located in the Vehicle Search panel.
  2. Click Search or press Enter on the keyboard.

The catalogue will be loaded, and the vehicle information will be displayed.

 

To retrieve a complete list of your Customer Tags, click the icon in the Vehicle Search panel.
