Add customer or account details

The customer and account details can be added to the Order List.

The customer details will be displayed on printouts, emails and saved orders. This allows you to provide a professional service.

 

To add customer details:

  1. Type the customer name into the Customer name box.
  2. Type the account or reference number into the corresponding boxes.
  3. Click the Customer tab to add information such as the address or contact details.

 

To save the Order List, add a customer name and click Save. To retrieve the saved Order List, click Saved Orders and select the required Order List.
