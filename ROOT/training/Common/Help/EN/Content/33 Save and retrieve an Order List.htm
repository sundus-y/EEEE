Save and retrieve an Order List

The Order List can be saved and retrieved at a later time.

This allows you to deal with more than one customer at a time. In addition, retrieving all saved orders at the end of the month is a useful way to follow-up on outstanding quotes.

This feature is only available online.

 

To save an Order List:

  1. Add customer or account details.

If you want to include more customer information, click the Customer tab and complete the information.

  1. Click Save.

The Order List will be saved in the Saved Orders panel.

 

To retrieve a saved Order List:

  1. Click Saved Orders on top of the Order List.
  2. Click the required Order List.

The Order List will be displayed in the Order tab. The customer information that was entered will be displayed in the Customer tab.

 

To delete a saved Order List, retrieve the Order List and click Delete at the bottom of the Order List.
