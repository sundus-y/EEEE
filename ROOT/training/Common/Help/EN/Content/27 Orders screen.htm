Orders screen

The Orders screen is used to manage your orders. You can save, print or email your Order List. In addition, you can transfer an Order List to your Dealer Management System (DMS).

To access the Orders screen at any time, click Orders at the top of the screen.

 

From the Orders screen you can:

  • View and edit an Order List
  • Change or hide pricing
  • Add a non-catalogued part
  • Add customer or account details
  • Group parts by vehicle name
  • Sort the list of parts on the Order List
  • Save and retrieve an Order List
  • Print or email an Order List
  • Transfer parts on the Order List to your DMS
