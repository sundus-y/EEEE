Use the image filmstrip

The image filmstrip allows you to preview the sections before or after the current section.

If the vehicle has been identified by VIN, the applicability of sections will be indicated in red or green.

 

To use the image filmstrip:

  1. Navigate to an image.
  2. Click anywhere in the image to activate the image area.
  3. Press Ctrl+Right Arrow on the keyboard.

The section number and title will be displayed on the title bar of the filmstrip.

  1. Press the Left or Right Arrow on the keyboard to preview the sections.
  2. Press Enter on the keyboard to select a section.

You can also use your mouse to select a section.

 

To show non-applicable sections, click General Settings from the Settings menu and select the Show non-applicable checkbox.
