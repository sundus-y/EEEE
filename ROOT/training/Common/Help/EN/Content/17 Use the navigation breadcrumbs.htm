Use the navigation breadcrumbs

The breadcrumbs are links at the top of your screen which allow you to easily navigate between the sections of the vehicle.

The breadcrumbs display your current location within the catalogue and the path to follow to go back to the Home page.

 

To use the breadcrumbs:

  1. Navigate to an image.
  2. Move the mouse over a breadcrumb.

A drop-down list of sections will be displayed.

  1. Select a section from the drop-down list.

 

To return to the Home page at any time, click Home.
