Adjust the image display

The image display can be adjusted to suit your preferences. In addition, you can print or email the image.

 

To adjust the image display, use the following options:

Display the image at actual size.

Resize the image to fit the page width.

Resize the image to fit the page height.

Print the image.

Send the image by email.

Drag the image in any direction. Ensure you click the icon to switch the option off.

Add a note for this section.

 

To go to the previous or next image in the catalogue, click the icons.
