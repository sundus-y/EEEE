Access supersession information

If a part has been superseded by other parts a supersession indicator will be displayed in the Parts Data Grid or Part Data window. A supersession search is useful to clear old stock or to keep up-to-date with the latest part information.

A supersession indicator consists of one or more characters and will vary based on your manufacturer. The indicator will be displayed in the OSI, INV or S/S column of the Parts Data Grid or in the corresponding field of the Part Data window.

 

To access supersession information:

  • Click the supersession indicator in the Parts Data Grid.
  • Click the supersession indicator in the Part Data window.

 

To conduct a supersession search:

  1. Click the supersession indicator.

The Search Supersession window will be displayed.

  1. View the supersession information.

The superseded part and the latest part information will be displayed.

  1. Click the checkbox to add the part to the Order List.
  2. Click Close.
