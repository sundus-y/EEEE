Print or email an Order List

Microcat allows you to print or email your Order List.

Emailing an Order List is useful to confirm an order with a customer or to streamline your day-to-day business operations.

 

To print an Order List:

  1. Add customer or account details.

If you want to include more customer information, click the Customer tab and complete the information. The details will be included in the printout.

  1. Click Print.

A preview of the printout will be displayed.

  1. Click the icon.

You can then fax the printout to your customer if required.

 

To email an Order List:

  1. Add customer or account details.

If you want to include more customer information, click the Customer tab and complete the information. The details will be included in the email.

  1. Click Email.

The Send Email window will be displayed.

  1. Type the email details, such as the email address, subject and message.

You can set the customer’s email reply to go to a different email address by typing it into the Reply to box. For example, the email address of your company or department.

  1. Click Send Email.
  2. Click OK.

 

To add a header and footer, click General Settings from the Settings menu and type the header and footer into the corresponding boxes.

To hide part numbers, click General Settings from the Settings menu and select the Hide part numbers checkbox.
