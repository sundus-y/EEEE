Add a group of parts to the Order List

You can use the lasso tool to select and add a group of several parts to the Order List at the same time. The lasso tool saves time, minimises errors and increases sales.

 

To add a group of parts to the Order List:

  1. Navigate to an image.
  2. Click and drag the mouse over the parts, then release the mouse.

When you select the group of parts ensure the intial click is not on a callout.

  1. Click Add to Order.

The group of parts will be added to the Order List.

The Part Option Resolver window will be displayed automatically if a selection between parts is required. For example, LH and RH.

 

To access the Orders screen at any time, click Orders at the top of the screen.

By default the recommended quantity of each part is added to the Order List. You can edit the quantity in the Qty box.
