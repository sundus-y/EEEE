Getting started

Microcat is the ultimate parts selling system.

 

Some of the benefits of using Microcat include:

  • The ability to provide the latest parts data
  • A streamlined user interface, navigation and workflow
  • Intuitive features for identifying a vehicle and searching for parts
  • Enhanced ability to integrate with your Dealer Management System

 

There are three main working areas in Microcat:

  • Home page
  • Parts screen
  • Orders screen
