Display a split-screen view

Microcat allows you to display a split-screen view when selecting parts. The split-screen view shows the Parts Data Grid on the left and the image on the right.

This is a useful way to see all the part data associated with an image. In addition, if the vehicle has been identified by VIN you can view the applicability of parts.

 

To display the split-screen view:

  1. Navigate to an image.
  2. Click the icon to display the split-screen view.

The Parts Data Grid will be displayed on the left and the image will be displayed on the right.

  1. View the applicability of each part:
  • Green: correct fit
  • Blue: selective fit
  • Red: incorrect fit

To show non-applicable parts, click General Settings from the Settings menu and select the Show non-applicable checkbox.

  1. Select a part in the Parts Data Grid or from the image.

 

To display the image above the Parts Data Grid, click the icon.
