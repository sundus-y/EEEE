Work with the Parts Data Grid

The Parts Data Grid displays part information in a table view below the image. You can easily switch between the Illustrated Parts tab and the Search Results tab.

This is useful as you can return to your latest search results by clicking the Search Results tab.

 

The Parts Data Grid consists of two tabs:

  • Illustrated Parts: Displays a list of illustrated sections and parts. Click the checkbox to add the part to the Order List. Click the description to display the Part Data window.
  • Search Results: Displays the search results after a search is performed. Click the icon to view the part in the image.

 

To adjust the Parts Data Grid, use the following options:

  • Click the icons on the title bar to show or hide the panel.
  • Double-click the title bar to show or hide the panel.
  • Click and drag the double-headed arrow up or down to adjust the height of the panel.

 

To adjust the column display:

  • Click the column heading to sort the column data.
  • Click and drag the column heading to move the column to a new position in the Parts Data Grid.
  • Click and drag the right edge of the column heading to the left or right to adjust the width of the column.
