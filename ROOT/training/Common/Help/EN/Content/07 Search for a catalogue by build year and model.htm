Search for a catalogue by build year and model

The catalogue search allows you to search for a vehicle by selecting the build year and model. You can then refine the search by selecting the major attributes.

The catalogue search is useful if the VIN is not known. The more major attributes that are entered the more accurate the selection of parts.

 

To search for a catalogue:

  1. Select the build year from the Year drop-down list.
  2. Select the vehicle catalogue from the Catalogue drop-down list.
  3. Select the major attributes from the drop-down lists in the Vehicle Information panel.

Use the Parts Search panel or the Graphic Index to find parts.

 

To clear the vehicle attributes, click Reset.
