Transfer parts on the Order List to your DMS

The Orders screen allows you to transfer parts on the Order List to your Dealer Management System (DMS). The Infomedia DMSi application must be installed and configured before transferring parts to your DMS.

Transferring parts to your DMS saves time, reduces errors and streamlines operations within your dealership.

 

To transfer parts on the Order List to your DMS (line by line):

  1. Add parts to the Order List.
  2. Click Transfer.

If your DMS is not running, you will be prompted to launch the application. Then, your DMS screen and the Transfer List window will be displayed.

  1. In the Transfer List window, select each part and click Transfer.

The parts will be transferred to your DMS.

 

To transfer parts on the Order List to your DMS at any time, click Transfer at the top of the screen. If you are using advanced integration, the Transfer button at the top of the screen may not be available.

For assistance with integrating Microcat with your DMS, contact Customer Service.

For more information on DMS integration options including Microcat Messaging Protocol (MMP), refer to the DMS Integration Guide.
