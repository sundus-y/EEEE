Add non-catalogued parts

Microcat allows you to easily add non-catalogued items to the Order List.

The Freeform part entry window allows you to include all items, such as workshop consumables, in the Order List. This is useful when creating a quote or invoice for a customer.

 

To add a non-catalogued part:

  1. Click Freeform part entry at the top of the Order List.
  2. Type the part number, description, quantity and price into the corresponding boxes.
  3. Click Add to Order.

The non-catalogued part will be added to the Order List.

 

To transfer non-catalogued parts to your DMS, the part numbers must be on record with your DMS.
